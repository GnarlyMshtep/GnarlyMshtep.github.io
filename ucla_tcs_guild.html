UCLA Theoretical Computer Science Guild

Howdy! We are UCLA's (first?) TCS community. During winter quarter, we will be meeting weekly to talk about lattices. We will mainly focus on lattice-based cryptography, but might touch up on the algorithms and complexity of lattices. We vaugly plan to go through (part of) the workshops graciously offered by the Simons institue and the course graciously put together by Vinod Vaikuntanathan. If for some crazy reason, that sounds like fun to you, please join our Discord Server! Hope to see you there!
