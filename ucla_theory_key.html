The UCLA Theory Key

The UCLA Theory key is a sacred artifact of the UCLA undergraduate theoretical computer science tradition. The purpose of the UCLA theory key is to recognize aspiring theoretical computer scientist at UCLA and encourege them to cultivate other aspiring theoreticians. The UCLA theory key is strongly linked to the UCLA Theory Guild. The key opens a room undergraduates do not have access to.

The UCLA Theory key is held by one undergraduate who has shown a commitment to obtaining a P.h.D. in theoretical computer science and has contributed to the undergraduate theory community. One the key holder graduates, he bequeathes the key to the next student, and so on.
